Overview

With integration of GIS, the organizing of data for the water distribution networks design, analysis and network management is simplified. GIS can contribute to decision making, damage control and planning for valve closing and opening, pipe break, service area managements. GIS empowers water networks with modern data model, out-of-the-box analytics tools, and easy-to-use maps, simplifying day-to-day operations.
